About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the GEODES Group at the University of Montreal within the Department of Computer Science and Operations Research. Under the guidance of Professor Houari Sahraoui , my research revolves around addressing challenges encountered by modelers during the software design process. Specifically, I delve into innovative approaches aimed at improving completion in domain modeling activities.

My research interests span several key areas, including Model-Driven Engineering, Language Models, Few-Shot Learning, Prompt Learning, Domain Modeling, Model Completion, and User Studies. These interests drive my passion for advancing these fields and enhancing the modeling process for both software engineers and researchers.
